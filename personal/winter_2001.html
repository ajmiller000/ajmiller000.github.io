WINTER 2001

Colorado


We skied in Colorado in March and Matteo took 9 days straight of snowboarding lessons. For the latter half of those, the small classes usually had kids who would slow things down too much for his tastes.


He could land about half of the big jumps like these.


He loved to cruise the trails among the trees which would usually finish with a jump. He also liked to makes jump-turns along the quarter-pipe sides of runs.
